### Variants

The variants for the secondary color are not fully supported yet. They may work in dark mode, but in light mode, the text color of secondary variant is not very visible.

#### Filled

There are four colors available for the filled variant `primary`, `accent`, `neutral` and `danger`.

<ComponentPreview className="flex-col md:flex-row">
  <Button variant="filled" color="primary">

  </Button>
  <Button variant="filled" color="accent">
    Accent
  </Button>
